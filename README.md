# Monte Carlo Lab

A growing Monte Carlo simulation lab. Modules are developed and refined iteratively.
---

## 🎯 Goal

Build intuition for probability and randomness through simulations.

---

## 🧠 Topics

- Law of Large Numbers
- Expected Value
- Random Walks
- Gambling / Risk Models
- Monte Carlo Estimation
- Portfolio Risk (basic)

---

## 🧪 Experimental Modules (Work in Progress)

Each module is implemented independently and improved over time.

### Week 1 – Coin Toss
Basic probability convergence

### Week 2 – Dice & Expected Value
Discrete distributions

### Week 3 – Pi Estimation
Geometric Monte Carlo

### Week 4 – Random Walks
Stochastic processes

### Week 5 – Gambling Ruin
Risk of bankruptcy

### Week 6 – Portfolio Risk
Basic financial simulation

## 🚀 Why this project?

Monte Carlo methods are widely used in:

- Quantitative finance
- Machine learning
- AI safety research
- Physics simulation

This project is a step toward understanding these systems from first principles.
